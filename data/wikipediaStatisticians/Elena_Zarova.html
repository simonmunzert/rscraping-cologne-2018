Elena Viktorovna Zarova (Russian: Елена Викторовна Зарова; born 16 May 1959) Doctor of Economics, Professor, Honored Scientist of the Russian Federation, International Statistical Institute, the Elected Member (ISI), Chair of ISI CEA Outreach Committee, Associated Reviewer of Statistical Journal of the IAOS (Journal of the International Association for Official Statistics).

Work experience[edit]

  • Present time – Analytical Centre by Moscow City Government, Head of Department; Professor of Statistics Department,Plekhanov Russian University of Economics
  • 2015 - 2016 - Director, Educational and Research Center "Statistics and Mathematics", Plekhanov Russian University of Economics
  • 2014 - 2015 - Vice-rector for Science, Professor of Statistics Department, Plekhanov Russian University of Economics
  • 2011 - 2014 - Professor of Statistics Department. Head of Statistics Department, Head of the Office of Research Department, Plekhanov Russian University of Economics
  • 2000 - 2011 - Vice-rector for Science and International Relations, Samara State Economic University
  • 1984 – 2000 – Assistant, Senior Lecturer, Associate Professor, Professor of Statistics Department, Dean of Agribusiness Department, Head of Statistics Department Disciplines: heory, business-statistics, international statistics, multivariate statistical analysis, time-series analysis and forecasting
  • Lecturing experience abroad: guest lecturer of Konstanz University, Germany (Applied Multivariate Statistical Analysis), 2014

Education, academic degrees and titles[edit]

  • 2000 – awarded the academic title of Professor of Statistics Department by the decision of Ministry of Education of the Russian Federation
  • 1999 - awarded the academic degree - Doctor of Economics
  • 1996 – 1998 – Doctoral studies, Statistics Department, Plekhanov Russian Academy of Economics
  • 1993 – awarded the academic title of Associate Professor, Statistics Department
  • 1984 – awarded the academic Ph.D. degree in Economics
  • 1980 – 1984 – Post-graduate studies, Statistics Department, Moscow Institute of National Economy named after G.V. Plekhanov
  • 1980 – Diploma with distinction, Kuibyshev Planning Institute, the specialty - industrial planning
  • 1976 – 1980 – student of the Faculty of Industrial Planning, Kuibyshev Planning Institute

International and public activity[edit]

  • Member of Scientific and Methodological Council of the Federal State Statistics Service
  • International Statistical Institute, Elected member[1]
  • Chair of ISI CEA Outreach Committee[2]
